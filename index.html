About Pizza Party Pickup

Pizza Party Pickup is a fun and addictive Disney pizza-making game where you play as a chef who needs to catch falling pizza ingredients to complete delicious pizzas. The game combines quick reflexes with strategy as you move your character across the screen to catch the right ingredients while avoiding unwanted items.

Originally created by Disney, Pizza Party Pickup has become a favorite among casual gamers for its simple yet challenging gameplay and colorful graphics. Now you can play this entertaining game for free right in your browser!

How to Play Pizza Party Pickup

  1. Use your mouse or finger (on mobile) to move the chef left and right
  2. Catch the correct pizza ingredients that fall from above
  3. Avoid catching rotten ingredients or other unwanted items
  4. Complete each pizza by collecting all required ingredients
  5. Score points for each correct ingredient caught
  6. Watch out for special power-ups that can help you!
